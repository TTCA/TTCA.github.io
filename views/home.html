The Telugu Cultural Association is an amalgamation of the Telugu speaking community in and around Calicut with an objective to promote, nurture and foster the rich Telugu culture, language, unique traditions and the talents of the students. We enable all the students, faculty and staff members of Telugu origin to enjoy, appreciate and be a part of our rich and colorful heritage. Since its origin the association has been successful in engaging masses from different backgrounds into the activities organized by TTCA throughout the origin.
