Academics

TTCA provides an opportunity where students are encouraged to explore the options which suits them the best, irrespective of the discipline to which they have been admitted. It works as an interlink between the faculty and students for financing new projects, startups and new talents.

TTCA provides means for the students to adjust to the new environment and culture and inform him/her about all policies and procedures of the college.
